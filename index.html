Why Manhattan?

Manhattan serves as New York City's economic and administrative center

Manhattan, the heart of the bustling metropolis known as New York City, is a place of enchantment and intrigue. From towering skyscrapers to vibrant neighborhoods, this iconic borough has captured the imagination of people from around the world. But beyond its recognizable skyline and renowned attractions, Manhattan is a treasure trove of astonishing facts that might just leave you speechless.

Manhattan is not only the financial capital of New York City but also the entire United States. It is home to Wall Street, the epicenter of the American financial industry, where the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) is located.
